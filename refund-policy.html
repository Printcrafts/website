Refund Policy

1. Overview

At PrintCraft, we strive to deliver high‑quality 3D prints every time. This Refund Policy explains when and how you may receive a refund, and which costs are non‑refundable.

2. Eligibility & Timeframes

  • Notification Window: All refund requests must be submitted within 14 days of receiving your package.
  • How to Request: Email support@printcraft.com with your Order Number, a clear description of the issue, and supporting photos.

3. Full Refunds

  • You may receive a 100% refund (including shipping fees) only under the following circumstances:
    1. Printer Malfunction or Operator Error: Your print failed due to a machine fault or handling error on our end (e.g., severe layer shifts, collapsed features), and we did not catch the defect during our pre‑shipment quality check.
    2. Damaged in Transit (Packaging/Carrier Error): Your part arrived damaged due to packaging or carrier mishandling, and the damage is not the result of model design flaws (e.g., thin walls or unsupported features).
    You must notify us within the 14‑day window and include photos of the defective or damaged item.

4. Non‑Refundable Situations

  • Refunds will not be granted for issues arising from user design choices or preferences. These include but are not limited to:
    • Design Flaws: Thin walls, missing holes, unsupported overhangs, or features that are structurally weak by design.
    • Support-Related Issues: We auto-generate supports where needed but do not manually remove or optimize them. If removal is difficult, damages the surface, or breaks your model during post-processing, this is a user-design responsibility.
    • Breakage from Structural Weakness: Fragile or intricate models that break in transit due to their own geometry or thin sections.
    • Misprint Due to Model Geometry: If the slicer accepted and generated valid toolpaths, we will print as-is—even if sagging, warping, or layer shifts occur because of the model’s design.
    • Material & Color Selection: Prints made in the chosen material or color that exhibit expected brittleness, flexibility, finish, or color variance.
    • Scale, Orientation & Export Settings: Unexpected results from chosen scale, orientation, file export settings (e.g., wrong units, corrupted mesh).
    • Surface Finish & Cosmetic Artifacts: Minor layer lines, texture variations, or small blemishes inherent to FDM printing.
    • Change of Mind or Incorrect Requirements: Any request due to second thoughts, wrong material choice, or altered specifications after printing begins.
    • Late Requests: Submitted after 14 days from the date of delivery.

5. Reprints & Revisions

  • For design corrections after delivery, place a new order at current pricing.
  • Free pre-print checks include flagging fatal slicer errors only; we do not provide free reprints for user-error models.

6. Processing Time

  • Approved refunds are processed within 7 business days.
  • Refunds return to your original payment method; banking processing times may vary.

7. Contact & Support

  • For questions or assistance with refunds, contact us:
  • Email: support@printcraft.com
