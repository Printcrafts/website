Our Story
PrintCraft was founded to bring transparent, on-demand 3D printing to hobbyists and pros alike. We believe you should only pay for what you use—and never for mistakes you didn’t make. That’s why every file is pre-checked for printability. If there’s an issue, we’ll let you know so you can correct it at no extra cost.
