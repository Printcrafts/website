FAQ
Printability Checks
How do you determine if my file is printable?+
We run every upload through our slicer and look for “hard” errors—like a solid region where you designed a hole, or non-manifold geometry that the printer simply can’t reproduce. Only those kinds of blocking issues get flagged, so you can go back, fix them, and re-upload without being charged extra.
What kinds of design problems won’t you catch?+
We do not QA minor aesthetic details or structural thinness. If your model has hair-thin walls or missing support elements, we’ll still slice and print it—as long as it doesn’t produce a fatal error in the slicer.
Will you add supports or thicken walls for me?+
No—your design is printed as submitted (plus our auto-generated supports as needed). We don’t take responsibility for whether your part’s walls are thick enough or whether you manually added support structures.
Design Responsibility
If my part fails or looks “wrong,” whose fault is it?+
Unless there was a slicer-blocking error we didn’t catch, any downstream print artifacts (layer shifts, sagging overhangs, weak features) stem from your model. We’ll alert you only to unprintable geometry—not aesthetic or mechanical shortcomings in your design.
What if my model is too thin or flexible?+
As long as the slicer can generate toolpaths, we’ll print it. Thin walls may break or warp in the real world, but we don’t modify your design or reject it on that basis.
Refunds & Shipping
What’s your refund policy?+
Partial refunds are available if you notify us of damage or major defects after printing—since we incur material and shipping costs. You do not need to ship the part back.

Full refunds (100%) are only granted if the error was on our end—for example, a machine malfunction that caused your figurine’s features to collapse or layers to severely delaminate.
How and when will I receive my refund?+
Once you contact us (ideally within 30 days of delivery) and we confirm damage or a genuine print-failure on our side, we'll process your refund promptly. Shipping fees are non-refundable in partial cases.
Do you send photos before shipping?+
Yes — after printing, we photograph your part and share those images so you can approve it (or flag visible defects) before we pack and ship.
